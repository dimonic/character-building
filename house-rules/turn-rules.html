Formula

Turn DC

Undead HD + Turn Resistance (if any)

Modifiers to roll

CL + Cha mod.

Total HD turnable per day:

(Cha mod. + 3) × CL

Where CL is the character's level in a class that has the "Turn Undead" class feature. If a feat uses one of your "turn undead" attempts such as Divine Might, subtract (Cha mod. + 3) from remaining "undead HD turnable" total.

Procedure

The player rolls a d20, adds charisma modifier + CL.

  1. Starting with the undead closest to the Cleric, and moving away, for each undead, determine if the HD of the undead would be within the character's remaining HD turnable for the day
  2. if yes, then:
    1. Determine the turn DC for the undead: (HD + turn resistance)
    2. Compare the PC's modified result with the DC: if equal or greater, the undead is turned, as usual, otherwise, the attempt has no effect
    3. Move on to the next undead in range
  3. if no, then skip this undead and move on to the next
